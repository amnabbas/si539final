Azores

It's a place where I imagine myself hiking through lush forests, breathing in the crisp, clean air, and discovering hidden waterfalls tucked away in the midst of nature's embrace. But it's not just about the scenery; the Azores offer a glimpse into a simpler way of life, where time seems to slow down and every moment feels like a treasure. From savoring local delicacies like freshly caught seafood to meeting friendly locals who welcome you with open arms, the Azores promise an authentic experience unlike any other.
